JARVIS V4 - Reliability + Undo + Writing Mode

New in V4:
- Screenshot privacy mode: internal screenshots go to data/screen_cache and auto-delete after SCREENSHOT_RETENTION_MINUTES.
- Manual screenshots still save normally in screenshots/.
- Screen target memory: OCR boxes and colored-button coordinates are cached so commands can still work after old screenshots are deleted.
- Color target clicking: say "press blue button" or "click blue button".
- Screen targets command: say "screen targets" or "what can you click".
- Command log: say "show command log".
- Undo: say "undo last action" after close tab or paste actions.
- Writing mode: say "start writing mode" and then dictate text. Say "new paragraph" or "stop writing mode".
- Better Google Docs paste flow: opens docs.new, waits, clicks document body, then pastes generated text.

Screenshot privacy design:
- Internal screenshots are needed for OCR/vision/clicking visible things.
- V4 does not need to keep every screenshot forever.
- It stores useful targets like text boxes and colored button coordinates in memory.
- Old internal screenshots are deleted automatically by default.
- To keep internal screenshots for debugging, set KEEP_INTERNAL_SCREENSHOTS=true in .env.
- To change retention time, set SCREENSHOT_RETENTION_MINUTES=15 or another number.
